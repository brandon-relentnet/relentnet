# STATEMENT OF WORK (SOW)

**Relentnet, LLC**  
Web Development & Application Development Services  
Nashville, Tennessee

---

## 1. Services Description

Relentnet provides professional **web development, application development, and digital product services** designed to deliver scalable, secure, and maintainable software solutions.

Services may include strategy, design, development, testing, deployment, and limited post-delivery support for websites, web applications, mobile applications, and custom software. Relentnet follows modern development standards and best practices to ensure performance, reliability, and long-term viability.

Unless expressly stated otherwise, Services under this SOW are **project-based** and do not include indefinite or ongoing maintenance, monitoring, or support.

---

## 2. Deliverables Description

Relentnet shall deliver the Services pursuant to the terms of this Statement of Work (“SOW”). This SOW defines the **scope of work, deliverables, and limited post-delivery services** to be provided.

### a. Core Development Deliverables

Relentnet shall provide design, development, and deployment services for the applications and platforms defined in the approved project scope, Project Agreement, accepted quote, or accepted invoice, which may include:

- Responsive website design and development
- Custom web application development
- Mobile application development (iOS, Android, cross-platform)
- API development and third-party integrations
- UI/UX design and front-end implementation
- Database architecture, design, and optimization
- Deployment, launch, and hosting configuration

All deliverables are developed using **licensed, vendor-supported frameworks, libraries, and platforms**. Relentnet assumes no liability for third-party software, services, themes, plugins, or assets supplied by the Client that are unlicensed, deprecated, unsupported, or unlawfully obtained.

If any third-party tools fall outside supported standards, Relentnet may recommend replacements or alternatives. Any third-party licensing fees or vendor charges require Client approval prior to being incurred.

---

### b. Limited Post-Delivery Support

All products delivered under this SOW include a **ninety (90) day limited support period**, beginning on the date of final delivery or production launch, whichever occurs first.

During this period, Relentnet may address:

- Bugs or defects directly attributable to Relentnet’s delivered code
- Functional issues preventing operation as originally specified

This limited support **does not include**:

- New features or enhancements
- Ongoing maintenance or monitoring
- Content updates or data changes
- Third-party service failures
- Issues caused by Client modifications, hosting changes, or external dependencies

---

### c. Ongoing Support & Maintenance

After the expiration of the 90-day support period, **no ongoing support or maintenance is included** unless the Client has entered into:

- A separate Relentnet Support & Maintenance Agreement, or
- A Relentnet Hosting Package that includes support services

Requests outside the included support period without an active agreement are **out of scope** and billed separately.

---

### d. No Implied Support

Delivery of any website, application, or software product **does not imply perpetual support, updates, or maintenance** unless expressly agreed to in writing.

---

## 3. Minimum Compliance Standards

- Client must provide timely access to required repositories, hosting platforms, credentials, and third-party services.
- Applications must operate on supported operating systems, browsers, and infrastructure.
- Security best practices are followed during development; ongoing operational security is a shared responsibility.

Relentnet shall not be responsible for delays, failures, service interruptions, data loss, or damages caused by **third-party platforms, vendors, APIs, hosting providers, SaaS services, or systems outside of Relentnet’s direct control**, or by Client negligence.

## 4. SOW Terms

### a. Project Phases & Implementation

Relentnet delivers projects using a **four-phase process** aligned with its published methodology.

#### Phase 1 — Discovery & Strategy

- Stakeholder alignment and requirements gathering
- Technical feasibility and architecture planning
- Platform, framework, and integration decisions
- High-level roadmap and milestone definition

#### Phase 2 — Design & Planning

- UI/UX wireframes and user flows
- Visual design and branding alignment
- Data modeling and system planning
- Finalized scope and build specifications

#### Phase 3 — Development & Implementation

- Front-end and back-end development
- Mobile application development (if applicable)
- CMS implementation (including WordPress where applicable)
- API and third-party integrations
- Version control, CI/CD pipelines, and internal QA

#### Phase 4 — Launch, Optimization & Transition

- Quality assurance and acceptance testing
- Client review and final approvals
- Production deployment and launch execution
- Post-launch stabilization
- Transition to limited post-delivery support or **separately contracted** ongoing services (if applicable)

**Acceptance Window:**  
Upon delivery of any milestone, build, or production deployment, the Client shall have **ten (10) business days** to review the deliverable and either provide written acceptance or submit written notice of **material nonconformity** with the agreed scope.

If the Client does not provide written notice within this review period, the deliverable shall be **deemed accepted**. Client review or feedback shall not delay payment obligations unless expressly agreed in writing by Relentnet.

## 6. Service and Fee Schedule

### a. Fees

Fees must be **expressly defined** in one or more of the following:

- This SOW
- A Project Agreement
- An accepted quote
- An accepted invoice issued by Relentnet

Any service or work not defined in one of the above documents is **out of scope**.

Invoices are issued in advance unless otherwise stated and are due by the invoice due date. Late payments may incur a **five percent (5%) monthly late fee** or the maximum permitted by law.

---

### b. Out-of-Scope (OOS) Work

Out-of-Scope work requires Client approval via a **Change Authorization Order (CAO)**, accepted quote, or accepted invoice and is billed separately.

---

### c. Term

This Agreement is effective for the **project duration**, which must be **expressly defined** in one or more of the following:

- This Statement of Work (SOW)
- A Project Agreement
- An accepted written quote
- An accepted invoice issued by Relentnet

If no project duration is explicitly defined, the project shall be deemed **open-ended solely for billing and administrative purposes** and shall continue until the defined scope is completed, this Agreement is terminated, or Relentnet elects to discontinue Services.

Any **ongoing or recurring services** shall be provided on a **monthly or annual recurring basis**, as selected by the Client and reflected on the invoice.

---

### d. Termination

#### 1. Termination by Relentnet

Relentnet may terminate this Agreement **at any time and for any reason**, as permitted by law. Relentnet may also suspend Services for non-payment, risk exposure, abuse, or operational concerns.

#### 2. Termination by Client

The Client may terminate with **thirty (30) days’ written notice**, subject to payment of all incurred fees, committed costs, and any applicable minimum terms. The Client may **not terminate for convenience once development has commenced**.

#### 3. Effect of Termination

All outstanding balances become immediately due. Services cease as of the termination date. Transition assistance is discretionary and billable.
